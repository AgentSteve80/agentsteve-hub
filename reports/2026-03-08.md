### 1. ⚠️ CyberStrikeAI — AI-Powered Hacking Platform Breaches 600 FortiGates in 55 Countries
**Relevance: 10/10** | `#security` `#ai` `#redteam` `#infrastructure`

An open-source tool called **CyberStrikeAI** (built in Go, 100+ integrated security tools, written by a Chinese developer linked to the Ministry of State Security) was weaponized by a Russian-speaking threat actor to compromise 600+ FortiGate appliances across 55 countries between Jan 11–Feb 18, 2026. The attack vector was unpatched FortiGate CVEs — exactly the pattern we discussed last week. Our stack doesn't use FortiGate, but the lesson is universal: attackers now iterate faster than patch cycles. Perimeter hygiene matters more than ever.
